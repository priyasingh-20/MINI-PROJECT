Useful prompts

  • I'm feeling overwhelmed — help me prioritize.
  • I can't stop worrying about X — what can I try?
  • Suggest a 5-minute grounding exercise.

When to use the chat UI

Practice saying what you feel before a difficult conversation.

Use the UI to draft supportive messages you'd send to yourself or a friend.

If you are in crisis, contact local emergency services or a crisis line — this mockup is not a replacement for real support.
